Color pattern evolution in butterflyfishes

 

We are currently investigating color pattern diversity and evolution in butterflyfishes (Chaetodontidae). Specifically, we are looking at how pattern conspicuousness and geometry relate to phylogeny, ecology, and behavior.

Collaborators: ME Alfaro, ST Schwartz, AJ Schultz

Publications

ME Alfaro, EA Karan, ST Schwartz, AJ Shultz. The Evolution of Color Pattern in Butterflyfishes (Chaetodontidae). Integrative and Comparative Biology, 2019.
